Projects

KubiRub

“KubiRub” (familiar to us as Rubik`s cube hand toy) will be launched as a 3D mobile game. Users can enjoy not only playing and relaxing, but also obtain bonuses and discounts from different companies. The mobile game is based on the main principle of Rubik`s cube but the faces of cubes consist of logos of companies instead of colors.

GardenX

GardenX is a smart plantpot that combines electronic and software solutions. With electronic components and sensors installed within soil, the pot provides autonomous watering of the plant according to its water level and sends the owner the necessary information on the plant via mobile application.

ComforTab

An innovative technological device, the ComforTab is a tablet that eases the working conditions of programmers and enables them to do programming anywhere they want to. Unlike other tablets, the ComforTab, which has 2 touchscreens, enables programmers to write codes on the first screen, while they see the codes on live preview on the second one. In other words, programmers will be able to do their work on one tablet anywhere instead of2-3 devices in one place.

BottleCheck

The project offers solutions for water supply to individual and corporate customers. When the electronic sensors that measure the level of water within the bottle identifies the decrease, the system send a new order the suppliers, providing information on the address and the number of bottles needed. In addition to the order, the necessary payment is also carried out through this system.

VeloRator

Innovative equipment for house and offices, the Velorator helps working people to save not only time when doing sports, but also generate electricity by themselves. The Velorator users, while doing their exercise, can work on electronic devices powered by alternative energy. The Velorator mobile application provides users with the necessary static information.

NoteFixation

The new system which is using the smart wrist bracelet will make people’s life even much easier. By using the smart wrist bracelet, you can easily convert what you say into an electronic note and you can add it to your daily plans. So, you can simply make a list of things to do, including things to do at home and things to buy outside for your home. You can easily get in touch with your family members through this mobile application. You can get additional tasks or to-do lists from them and even you are able to edit these tasks. Additionally, there is a feature called System Deep Learning which creates a list of daily things to do and products to be purchased in terms of what you have done and purchased so far daily. The system are able to be operated by both wrist bracelet and mobile application independently.

CardPlus - Mobile Game

What is this

“CardPlus” is a simple addicted 2D mobile game. This mobile game consists of 9 uncolored cubes and when you tap one of these cubes you are able to see the color of the cube. The main idea is to get more scores by finding 3 same-colored cubes consequently. As it is seen, you need a bit success and logic in order to win the game. Enjoy it by downloading it from Google Play Store and AppStore.
